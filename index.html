60-Second Tool Selection Checklist
  • Data Sensitivity: Prefer 🔒 local/on-prem for sensitive data.
  • Evidence & Transparency: Favor tools that provide citations (✅).
  • Academic Integration: Exports and compatibility (RIS/BibTeX/CSV/PDF; Zotero/Overleaf/LMS).
  • Cost & Access: Look for 🔁 free tiers or 🏫 EDU plans.
  • Maturity & Support: Stable projects vs 🧪 betas.
  • Ethics & Compliance: Verify outputs; disclose AI assistance.
